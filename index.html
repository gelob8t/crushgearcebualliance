About the alliance

Made for enthusiasts nga taga Cebu.

Crush Gear Cebu Alliance is a fan-led community for hobbyists who love the excitement of gear battles, creative builds, and nostalgia-driven collecting. Whether you are returning to the hobby or just discovering it, this community helps you learn, join, and battle with confidence.

This website is an unofficial fan-community page and is not affiliated with Bandai, Sunrise, or the original Crush Gear rights holders.

Build hub

Setups, repairs, and tuning notes

⚙

Starter setup

Choose a stable chassis, test wheel grip, confirm battery fit, and inspect weapon alignment before battle.

⟲

Maintenance

Clean axles, remove dust, check loose screws, and test rolling balance after every meetup.

✦

Custom parts

Use safe materials, avoid sharp exposed edges, and make sure modified parts are secure before entering the arena.

☑

Event checklist

Bring gear, batteries, tools, spare wheels, tape, container box, charger, and your best sportsmanship.
